# Demo Rescue Playbook

## When To Use

Use when the live demo is unstable, time is low, or the team is not sure what can be safely shown.

## Step 1: Identify What Works

List only features that work now.

## Step 2: Pick One Story

Choose the clearest path through the product.

## Step 3: Replace Risk With Artifacts

Use:
- seeded data
- screenshots
- video clips
- generated outputs
- static mocks

## Step 4: Rewrite Pitch Honestly

Say what works.
Do not claim broken features are live.

## Step 5: Freeze

No new features.
No broad refactors.
Only demo-saving patches.
