# Recommended Linear Views

## Demo Critical

Filter:
- label: demo-critical
- status is not Cut

Use:
The main board during the sprint.

## Blocked

Filter:
- status: Blocked
- or label: blocked

Use:
Unblock teammates quickly.

## Ready To Pick Up

Filter:
- status: Ready

Use:
Find next work.

## Needs Review

Filter:
- status: Needs Review

Use:
Verify completed work.

## Verified

Filter:
- status: Verified

Use:
See what is safe.

## Cut List

Filter:
- status: Cut

Use:
Prevent re-opening dead scope.

## Final 6 Hours

Filter:
- priority: P0/P1
- status is not Demo Ready or Cut

Use:
Late-stage execution.

## Owner: Me

Filter:
- assignee: current user

Use:
Personal work queue.
