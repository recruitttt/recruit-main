# Demo Plan

## Demo Thesis

AI Study Buddy turns messy course notes into a focused final-exam plan in under one minute.

## Audience

Hackathon judges and students.

## Live Click Path

1. Open upload screen.
2. Paste sample biology notes.
3. Click Generate Plan.
4. Show cram plan, weak areas, and practice questions.
5. Explain next study block.

## Scripted Fallback

If live generation fails, show seeded dashboard screenshot and 45-second recorded successful path.

## Screenshots / Videos Needed

- Upload screen
- Loading state
- Generated dashboard
- Practice questions

## What Not To Claim

- PDF support works live
- Calendar integration exists
- Results are perfect tutoring advice

## Closing Line

AI Study Buddy gives overwhelmed students a clear next hour of studying when they need it most.
